Why JNPR? The name comes from “Juniper Berries”, the juniper berry in English, which is the main ingredient of Gin, but also that of the JNPR range. We don't have vowels, just like we don't have alcohol. As soon as the adventure was launched, plants of these famous junipers were replanted in Normandy, right next to the JNPR offices.
